Lesson 6 – Creating Basic Visualizations & Adding Interactivity to Visuals (Nazariy javoblar)

1. Name three types of visuals you can create in Power BI.
   → Bar chart, Column chart, Line chart.

2. How do you add a slicer to a report?
   → Visualizations paneldan "Slicer" ni tanlab, kerakli field (masalan Quarter) ni qo‘shasiz.

3. What is the difference between a bar chart and a column chart?
   → Bar chart – ma’lumotlar gorizontal ko‘rinishda chiqadi.
     Column chart – ma’lumotlar vertikal ko‘rinishda chiqadi.

4. How do you change the color of a visual background?
   → Visual tanlanadi → Format panel (Paint roller belgisi) → Background → rangni tanlash.

5. What does "drill-down" mean in a visual?
   → Drill-down – bu ierarxiya ichida chuqurlashib, yuqori darajadagi ma’lumotdan pastki darajaga o‘tish 
     (masalan, Region → Product → Quarter).
