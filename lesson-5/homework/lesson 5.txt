Lesson 5 – Data Modeling Basics (Nazariy javoblar)

1. What is a primary key in a table?
   → Primary key – bu jadvaldagi har bir satrni noyob qilib ajratib turuvchi ustun 
     (masalan, Customer jadvalidagi CustomerID).

2. Name the two types of table relationships in Power BI.
   → One-to-Many (1:*), Many-to-Many (*:*).

3. What is a "star schema"?
   → Star schema – bu bitta markaziy fact jadval (odatda Sales) va uning atrofida dimension jadvallari 
     (Customer, Product, Date) bo‘ladigan model.

4. Which table is typically the fact table in a sales dataset?
   → Sales jadvali fact table hisoblanadi.

5. Why is ProductID in Sales.csv a foreign key?
   → Chunki u Products jadvalidagi Primary key (ProductID)ga bog‘lanadi va shu orqali mahsulot ma’lumotlari 
     Sales jadvaliga ulanadi.

6. Explain why a star schema improves performance.
   → Chunki jadval tuzilishi sodda, filtrlash tez ishlaydi, murakkab joinlardan qutiladi 
     va xotira samaraliroq foydalaniladi.

7. Use bidirectional filtering sparingly—when is it appropriate?
   → Faqat juda zarur bo‘lganda, masalan, ko‘p-jadvalli ko‘rsatkichlarni hisoblashda 
     yoki "many-to-many" vaziyatlarda bidirectional filtering ishlatiladi.
